The Food Truck

Same kitchen, on wheels

The truck runs a focused version of the menu — empanadas, arepas, a Go Bowl, and Pabellón Criollo — built for fast service at events and pop-ups around the Valley. Truck pricing is set on-site and may differ from the restaurant.

  • Private bookings follow the same $1,000 minimum and two-week lead time as catering.
  • For public appearances and pop-up locations, follow us on Instagram or Facebook.
