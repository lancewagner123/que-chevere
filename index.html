Our Story

Started by Maria Fernanda and Orvid in 2016

Maria Fernanda brought her family's Venezuelan cooking to Arizona from Barquisimeto, Venezuela — Orvid tasted it and knew it needed to reach more people. Que Chevere has run as a restaurant and a food truck ever since: same recipes, same kitchen, two ways to get to you.

  • Dine in or takeout at 142 W Main St, Mesa — open Wed–Sat and Sunday.
  • Food truck menu of empanadas, arepas, and bowls — schedule varies, so follow our socials for where we'll be.
  • Catering for groups — $1,000 minimum, two weeks' notice. See catering details →
